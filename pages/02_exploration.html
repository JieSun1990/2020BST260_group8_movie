Movie Exploration

How to Visualize the Movie Industry?

Movie, also named film, is a visual art that utilizes moving images to communicate ideas, perceptions, feelings, stories, beauty and atmosphere, accompanied with sound, music, and aftereffects. Movie becomes as one of the necessary parts of modern peoples entertainments; however, have you ever visualized the data of the movies based on their features, such as genre, year, or box office? IMDb is one of the largest movie data websites that has all the information we need for such visualization; therefore, our data set included the movie data between 2020-2020 was obtained from IMDb.

Related Work:

Since our data is about movie and our purpose is to explore such data and figure out some analysis related to such dataset, Shiny app becomes an appropriate tool for us to make such dataset visualized. Moreover, in the Shiny app gallery, we found one perfect instance for our project.Movie Explorer. In such example, the data is from a subset of data from the OMDb that is saved in an SQLite database and came from IMDb and Rotten Tomatoes.

The major function for this Shiny app is to display a plot with information that selected by the users; for instance, the users can choose the released year, number of Oscar wins, or genre of the movies and the app will make a relative plot based on those selections, which we also have discussed in the class. Moreover, the users can select the x-axis and y-axis that corresponding to different variables in the database. Besides, the users can search for specific director or cast members and the app will filter such movies with information on the plot.

Therefore, we were inspired by such work and decided to achieve similar functions that included in this app by our own dataset and try to understand the method to produce a successful movie.
